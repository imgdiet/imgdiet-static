Online Image Processing Tool

Imgdiet is an online image compression and image conversion tool. It is based on local browser JavaScript technology to compress and convert images, so the images never leave your device and the data is 100% secure. The tool supports custom compression rates, allowing you to compress image quality from "1% to 99%". It also supports lossless compression for PNG and WebP formats. It can convert dozens of image formats such as JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, TIF, DDS, AVIF, PSD, HEIC, RAW, and JFIF into PG, JPEG, and PNG images.
